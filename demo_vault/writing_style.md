# Writing Style

Notes are most useful when they are atomic, linked, and written in your own words.

## Guidelines
- One idea per note. Split when a note grows two distinct topics.
- Link generously with [[wikilinks]] — links are cheap and make the
  [[backlinks_graph]] valuable.
- Add `tags:` in frontmatter for cross-cutting themes that links don't capture.
- Prefer plain language; future-you is the primary reader.

Good notes compound: a well-linked vault becomes searchable by both keyword and
[[semantic_search]].
